OUR AMENITIES

POOL & JACCUZI

The new infinity swimming pool has a total surface area of 1200 sqm, with a water volume of 1000 cubic meters and a maximum depth of 1.2 meters. Designed for families, honeymooners and friends travelling together, the pool features a stunning open layout and unique details. Families with young children will have the option of a dedicated kids section which measures 0.5 meters in depth and is an ideal spot for young ones to practice their swimming skills while enjoying the best of the Maldives

CHI SPA

Chi, The Spa is committed to providing a relaxing atmosphere for all who enter. Massages and treatments are based on authentic natural healing methods found in traditional well-being practices shared by many Asian cultures. CHI, The Spa also offers Ayurvedic treatments that reflect the essence of India, such as Abhyanga, Udwartana, Pinda Sweda and Shirodhara.

THE TARA BEACH

The resort is situated at a walking distance of around 700 meters from the Tara beach. The Tara beach could be spanned by rental bikes and bicycles so that you don't have to go by any other means and enjoy the salty air in the area. Definitely a fun experience!

BUFFET HALL

We are proud to provide an array of excellent dining options. Our guests can enjoy International, Chinese, Italian, Japanese and Indian dishes, and much more. The spaciousness of the hall accomodates all the guests in a luxurious way.

OUTDOOR CAFE

An incredible culinary adventure awaits at Cafe Era, right outside the vicinity tour area. From award-winning Italian and authentic Cantonese fare to inspired barrel-aged cocktails and classic chai served with a modern twist, we invite you to craft your own unique gastronomic journey.

SKYLINE BALCONY

Accented by unique curvatures and original local artworks, the topmost layer of our resort offer sublime views of Maldive's famous skyline and breath-taking sunset view. Shades of grey, white and gold provide both a soothing and stylish evening, while the off-white wooden-clad floors feature an aesthetic finish to the balcony.

VICINITY TOUR

A private tour of the vicinity lets you visit numerous monasteries, stunning natural sites, and gorgeous vista points without the rush and bustle of a group tour. Your guide escorts you and your select companions to Maldives' must-see destinations.
