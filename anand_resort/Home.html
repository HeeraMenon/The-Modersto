SLEEP & WELLBEING

At Modersto we value your sleep just as much as you do.

We want to offer you the most relaxing oasis in the heart
of Maldives. Our rooms are soundproof, and we only use
the highest quality materials. We also make sure there are
no distractions in the rooms, such as TV and electronics,
to improve your sleep quality. We strive to reduce waste,
so we don't keep plastic bottles and glasses in our room.
However you can find a fully stocked vending machine in
the lounge. Hairdryers, irons and ironing boards are
available from reception (subject to availability and holding
deposit)
